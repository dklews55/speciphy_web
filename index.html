Standards Contributions

IEEE 802.3

Ethernet Standards

Contributed to high-speed Ethernet standards including 100G, 400G, and emerging 800G optical interfaces. Member of the IEEE 802.3 Ethernet Working Group.

OIF

Optical Internetworking Forum

Active participant in OIF projects for coherent optics, pluggable modules, and multi-vendor interoperability. Contributed to CEI-56G and 112G electrical interfaces.

ITU-T

International Standards

Contributor to ITU-T optical transport standards including G.709 OTN and G.698 Raman amplification. Involved in international optical standards harmonization.

MSAs

Multi-Source Agreements

Technical contributor to numerous optical MSAs including QSFP-DD, OSFP, and COBO. Led interoperability testing and specification development for pluggable optics.
